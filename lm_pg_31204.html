Which English?

Which English is your English?

Do you know whether you speak/write American or Britiish English?
Both versions are completely fine! However, it is useful to know which one comes more naturally to you and then to follow the rules of that one in your writing and speaking. It is better to stick to one language rather than mix them. Consistency is key!
